About

Terocollege.com is a soon-to-be-launched startup by USA, UK and India educated students ; established with an aim of providing unbiased information about colleges and Institutes all over the world to Nepalese students. Tero in Nepali translates to “Tera” in Hindi and “Yours” in English. Terocollege.com will act as a perfect digital platform to bring together prospective students and colleges so that students will get all the information at the click of their mouse without depending upon the middlemen to get admitted into the colleges of their choice.
